Last updated: March 24, 2026

Daily One Piece is accessible through the dailyonepiece.com domain. For any question related to personal data protection, you can contact: contact@dailyonepiece.com.

When you use the site, we may collect the following data:

  • email address;

Your data is used to:

4. Legal basis

Processing is based, depending on the case, on the performance of the requested service, compliance with legal obligations, the site's legitimate interest in securing its operation, and your consent for non-essential cookies, especially advertising cookies.

The site may integrate advertising partners such as ad networks. These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • display ads;
  • limit ad repetition;
  • measure campaign performance;
  • personalize ads when you consent to this.

Strictly necessary cookies may be stored without consent. Non-essential analytics cookies and advertising cookies require your choice through the cookie banner.

6. Data recipients

Data may be processed by technical service providers used by the site, including:

  • hosting and deployment providers;
  • database and authentication providers;
  • traffic and analytics tools;
  • future advertising partners.

7. Retention period

Account data is kept as long as the account remains active, then for the period required to comply with legal obligations or manage disputes. Cookie data is stored according to its purpose and applicable regulations.

8. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, alteration or disclosure.

Under the GDPR, you may request:

  • access to your data;
  • correction of your data;

  • withdrawal of your consent when processing relies on it.

To exercise your rights, contact: contact@dailyonepiece.com.
